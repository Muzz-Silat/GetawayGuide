What data do we collect?

Getaway Guide collects the following data:
-Personal identification information for login (Name, email address, phone number, etc.)
-Save personal credentials (passwords)
-Profile preferences (meal preferences, gender, marital status, allergies etc.)
-Option to save card details for future purchases How do we collect your data?

You directly provide GetawayGuide with most of the data we collect. We collect data and process data when you:

-Register online and make an account, verification
-Voluntarily complete a customer survey or provide feedback on our services during previous trips that the user had
-Use or view our website via your browser’s cookies.
-User input of preferences, enters plans of itineraries etc.

GetawayGuide may also receive your data indirectly from the following sources:

-Ads
-Partner websites
How will we use your data?

GetawayGuide collects your data so that we can:

-Implement your plans and manage your account.
-Email you with special offers on other trips and services we think you might like, based on your previous trips and input preferences.
-Data mining for getting places and countries accustomed to your liking with relevant sightseeing tours.

If you agree, GetawayGuide will share your data with our partner companies so that they may offer you their products and services.

-Skyscanner flights
-Tripadvisor hotels
-YellowPages

When GetawayGuide works on your purchase, it may send your data to, and also use the resulting information from, credit reference agencies to prevent fraudulent purchases.
How do we store your data?

GetawayGuide securely stores your data at our in house database, secured via multi factor authentications. Our servers will be hosted by the University of Birmingham - the servers are in Europe/the EEA.

GetawayGuide will keep your personal data for 10 years. Once this time period has expired, we will delete your data by replacing all credentials with null and deleting the user account. If the user wishes to continue activation of their account, they would be prompted via email to renew their time period and re-enter their details that may need to be updated.

What are your data protection rights?

GetawayGuide would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

The right to access – You have the right to request GetawayGuide for copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.

The right to rectification – You have the right to request that GetawayGuide correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request GetawayGuide to complete the information you believe is incomplete.

The right to erasure – You have the right to request that GetawayGuide erase your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that GetawayGuide restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to GetawayGuide’s processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to data portability – You have the right to request that GetawayGuide transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our

Cookies

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our websites, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology
For further information, visit allaboutcookies.org.

How do we use cookies?

GetawayGuide uses cookies in a range of ways to improve your experience on our website, including:
Keeping you signed in
Understanding how you use our website
How we can improve your experience in terms of accessibility.
Help us understand which parts of our Web site interest our visitors and if they experience any errors.


What types of cookies do we use?

There are a number of different types of cookies, however, our website uses:
-Functionality – Getaway Guide uses these cookies so that we recognize you on our website and remember your previously selected preferences. These could include what language you prefer and location you are in. A mix of first-party and third-party cookies are used.
-Advertising – Getaway Guide uses these cookies to collect information about your visit to our website, the content you viewed, the links you followed and information about your browser, device, and your IP address. Getaway Guide sometimes shares some limited aspects of this data with third parties for advertising purposes. We may also share online data collected through cookies with our advertising partners. This means that when you visit another website, you may be shown advertising based on your browsing patterns on our website.
-Analytical cookies to help improve on errors you face or your most liked and disliked pages.

How to manage cookies

You can set your browser not to accept cookies, and the above website tells you how to remove cookies from your browser. However, in a few cases, some of our website features may not function as a result.
Privacy policies of other websites

The Getaway Guide website contains links to other websites. Our privacy policy applies only to our website, so if you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.
